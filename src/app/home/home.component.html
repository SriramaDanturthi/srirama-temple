About 108 Divya Desam Yatra

In Vaishnavism around 5000 years back there were gurus called Alwars. There were 12 gurus called Alwars who visited all 106 holy places. Alwars visited each place and they composed songs in the praise of the lord which are familiar as Naalayiram divya prabandham in Tamil language. The meaning of “Naalayiram” Tamil word indicates the number 4000 pasurams. Read more
